Frequently Asked Questions

Are your recipes suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! Every recipe includes detailed step-by-step instructions, exact measurements, and helpful tips for first-timers. Whether you're making sourdough bread for the first time or attempting butter chicken, we walk you through each step clearly.

How do you calculate the nutrition information?

We calculate nutrition data using standard USDA food composition databases for each ingredient. Calorie counts, protein, carbohydrates, fat, and fiber are provided per serving. These are estimates and may vary slightly based on specific brands and ingredient sizes you use.

Can I substitute ingredients in your recipes?

Yes! Most recipes include a "Tips & Variations" section with specific substitution ideas. For example, our Buddha Bowl works with brown rice instead of quinoa, and our focaccia can be topped with whatever herbs you have on hand.

Are the vegan recipes completely plant-based?

Yes, all recipes in our vegan collection are 100% plant-based — no dairy, eggs, honey, or any animal-derived ingredients. They're also naturally rich in protein from sources like chickpeas, lentils, quinoa, and tofu.

How spicy are the Indian recipes?

We rate our Indian recipes as medium spice by default. Every recipe includes guidance on adjusting heat levels — you can reduce chili powder for a milder version or add extra green chilies for more kick. Our Dal Makhani is naturally mild, while Chana Masala has more heat.

Can I meal prep your recipes?

Most of our recipes are excellent for meal prep. Curries like Butter Chicken and stews like our Lentil Stew actually taste better the next day. We include storage tips and reheating instructions where applicable.
