About Our Farm Kitchen

Our Story & Philosophy

Born in a Farmhouse Kitchen

A Farm Recipe started as handwritten recipe cards passed down through three generations of home cooks. What began around a single kitchen table in the countryside has grown into a community of thousands who believe great food starts with great ingredients.

Triple-Tested, Always Honest

Every recipe on this site is tested at least three times before publishing. We measure every ingredient, time every step, and photograph the real results — no food styling tricks, no impossible techniques. If it doesn't work in a home kitchen, it doesn't make it to our site.

Seasonal, Sustainable, Simple

We choose ingredients for flavor, nutrition, and sustainability. Our recipes celebrate what's in season — from spring asparagus to autumn pumpkins. We believe cooking should be accessible, joyful, and rooted in the rhythms of the land.

Our Team

Home cooks, food photographers, and nutrition enthusiasts who share a love for honest, simple food. We come from different culinary traditions — Indian, Italian, and plant-based — united by a belief that the best meals come from the freshest ingredients, cooked with care.
